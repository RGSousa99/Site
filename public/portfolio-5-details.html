CineFilme

This work aims to apply the knowledge acquired regarding User Experience design during the software development cycle such-as-requirements gathering, the impact of human factors on interaction design and prototyping for evaluation with users, to a smartphone application. This application will have the scope to provide information to users about movies, such as: the title; brief description (synopsis), release date, score, among other pertinent information.
